Hotel Danieli

Hotel Danieli is a five star luxury hotel built in 14th-century by one of the Donaldo families. It has been a hotel since 1822. The main feature is four-storied courtyard which is covered with arches in Byzantine Gothic style.The hotels 233 rooms and suits are spread throught central building with three wings. The Doges suit is the most luxurious.Furniture is from 18th-century. The rooftop terrace restaurant has view of lagoon and Venice.
